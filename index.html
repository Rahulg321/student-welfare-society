Buy Food!

For this punishment the late comer would have to buy a food item for the whole class. Keep in mind the food item need to be of the person's choosing.

Story Telling!

For this punishment the late comer would have to narrate an embarrasing story about themselves to the whole class. The story and context can be of the teacher's choosing.

Stage Acting!

For this punishment the late comer has to act out a scene from a movie. Keep in mind the scene would be the choice of the concerned teacher.
